About

It is entirely student led and student taught, so you'll get the opportunity to work closely with people who actually use what they teach at hackathons, school, and in their careers.

We have meetings on Wednesdays from 5:00pm to 6:00pm in the UC Pake Room. Feel free to stop in, hear what we're working on, and help out!

To learn more about our organization, and about how you can get involved, email info@scottylabs.org. To learn about how you can sponsor ScottyLabs, email sponsor@scottylabs.org. You can also find us on Facebook and Github.
